Machine Learning and Programming Enthusiast

Hi! Nice to meet you. Welcome to my Portfolio.

My background is in computer science and I have been working mainly in the field of Data Science and Machine Learning, implementing and serving intelligent solutions. I have used classical and deep learning techniques, although due to the solutions nature I have been experimenting mostly with the latter, more precisely for tasks related to computer vision (CV) and natural language processing (NLP).

Previous and non-professinal experience include implementing optimization solutions using genetic algorithms, SAT and MIP technologies, and the deployment and maintenance of a high performance computing cluster.

I mostly use Python as it is the de facto language for ML tasks and has plenty of libraries to implement solutions for other domains, but I also enjoy working with other languages and frameworks that look promising.
